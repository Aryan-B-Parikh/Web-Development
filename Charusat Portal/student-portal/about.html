About CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology

Our Story

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is a premier institution established in 2000, dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering innovation. Located in Changa, Gujarat, India, CHARUSAT has grown from a single institute to a comprehensive university with multiple faculties and departments.

Our university is committed to academic excellence, research innovation, and societal development. We believe in nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow through quality education and holistic development.
